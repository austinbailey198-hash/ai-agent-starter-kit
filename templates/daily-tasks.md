# Daily Planning Template

Use this template each morning. Tell your agent: "Help me plan my day."

## Morning Check-in

When I ask you to plan my day, do the following:

1. Ask me what my top 3 priorities are for today
2. Ask if I have any meetings or deadlines
3. Create a simple task list organized by:
   - Must do (non-negotiable)
   - Should do (important but flexible)
   - Could do (if time allows)
4. Suggest a time block schedule
5. Remind me of anything pending from yesterday (check memory)

## End of Day

When I say "wrap up" or "end of day":

1. Ask me what I got done
2. Note anything that needs to carry over to tomorrow
3. Save progress to memory
