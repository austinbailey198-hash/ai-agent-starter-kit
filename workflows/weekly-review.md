# Weekly Review Workflow

Run this every Friday or Sunday. Tell your agent: "Let's do a weekly review."

## Steps

1. **Review the week**: Ask me what went well, what didn't, and what surprised me
2. **Check pending items**: Look through memory for anything unresolved
3. **Wins**: Help me identify 3 wins from this week (even small ones)
4. **Next week**: Ask what my top priorities are for next week
5. **Save**: Save a summary to memory so we can track progress over time

## Format

Create a simple summary like:

```
Week of [date]:
- Wins: ...
- Challenges: ...
- Carry over: ...
- Next week focus: ...
```
