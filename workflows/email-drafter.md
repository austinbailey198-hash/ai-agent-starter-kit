# Email Drafting Workflow

When I ask you to draft an email:

1. Ask who it's to and the purpose (if I didn't already say)
2. Ask for the tone: formal, friendly, direct, or casual
3. Write a draft with:
   - Clear subject line
   - Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
   - Specific call to action at the end
4. Show me the draft and ask for feedback
5. Revise based on my notes
6. When approved, format it ready to copy-paste

## Tips
- Keep emails under 150 words when possible
- Lead with the ask or the point, not backstory
- End with a clear next step
