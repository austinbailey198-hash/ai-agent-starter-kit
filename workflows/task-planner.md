# Task Planner Workflow
### Prompt Person™ AI Agent Starter Kit

---

## When to Use
When you want to organize your day, prioritize tasks, or plan your week. Best used in the morning or at the start of a work session.

## How It Works

### Step 1: Gather
Ask the user: "What's on your plate today?" or "What do you need to get done this week?"
Collect all tasks, meetings, deadlines, and commitments.

### Step 2: Categorize
Sort tasks into:
- **Must do** — Hard deadlines, meetings, time-sensitive items
- **Should do** — Important but flexible timing
- **Could do** — Nice to have, no deadline pressure

### Step 3: Prioritize
Order tasks within each category by impact. What moves the needle most?
Flag anything that's been sitting too long and needs attention.

### Step 4: Time Block
Suggest a rough schedule:
- Morning: Deep work / most important task
- Midday: Meetings, calls, collaborative work
- Afternoon: Follow-ups, admin, lighter tasks
- End of day: Review and prep for tomorrow

### Step 5: Follow Up
At the end of the day (or next session), ask:
- "What did you get done today?"
- "Anything rolling over to tomorrow?"
Save unfinished tasks to memory for next time.

---

## Example Prompts
- "Help me plan my day"
- "What should I focus on today?"
- "I have 5 things to do — help me prioritize"
- "Plan my week — I have a deadline Friday and a client call Tuesday"
- "What's still on my plate from yesterday?"
