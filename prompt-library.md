# Prompt Person™ — 50+ Tested Prompt Library

These prompts work with any AI agent but are optimized for Claude. Copy-paste them directly or customize the bracketed fields.

---

## EMAIL & COMMUNICATION

1. "Draft a cold outreach email to [PERSON/COMPANY]. I'm a [YOUR ROLE] looking to [YOUR GOAL]. Keep it under 100 words, friendly but professional."

2. "Write a follow-up email to [NAME] — I reached out [X days] ago about [TOPIC] and haven't heard back. Keep it short and not pushy."

3. "Draft a thank-you email to [NAME] for [WHAT THEY DID]. Make it genuine, not generic."

4. "I need to deliver bad news to [NAME] about [SITUATION]. Draft an email that's honest, empathetic, and offers a path forward."

5. "Write a professional response to this email: [PASTE EMAIL]. My position is [YOUR STANCE]."

6. "Draft a meeting request email to [NAME]. I want to discuss [TOPIC]. Suggest 2-3 time slots this week."

7. "Write an introduction email connecting [PERSON A] and [PERSON B]. Context: [WHY THEY SHOULD MEET]."

8. "Draft an apology email to [NAME] about [SITUATION]. Take responsibility without over-apologizing."

9. "Write an email asking [NAME] for feedback on [PROJECT/WORK]. Make it easy for them to respond."

10. "Draft a newsletter intro about [TOPIC]. Hook readers in the first line, keep it under 150 words, end with a teaser for what's inside."

---

## RESEARCH & ANALYSIS

11. "Research [COMPANY] and give me a 5-bullet summary: what they do, target market, recent news, main competitors, and anything I should know before my meeting with them on [DATE]."

12. "Compare [PRODUCT A] vs [PRODUCT B] vs [PRODUCT C]. Give me a comparison table with pricing, key features, pros, cons, and your recommendation for someone who [MY USE CASE]."

13. "What are the top 5 trends in [INDUSTRY] right now? For each one, explain what it means and whether I should care as a [YOUR ROLE]."

14. "Summarize this article in 3 bullet points. Focus on what's actionable for someone in [YOUR FIELD]: [PASTE ARTICLE OR URL]"

15. "Give me the pros and cons of [DECISION]. I'm trying to decide between [OPTION A] and [OPTION B]. My priorities are [LIST PRIORITIES]."

16. "Fact-check this claim: [CLAIM]. Is it accurate? What's the actual data?"

17. "Research the market size for [INDUSTRY/NICHE]. Who are the major players? What's the growth trajectory?"

18. "Compare pricing models in [INDUSTRY]. What do companies typically charge for [SERVICE/PRODUCT]?"

19. "Analyze this data and tell me what stands out: [PASTE DATA OR DESCRIBE IT]"

20. "Give me a literature review on [TOPIC]. What are the key findings from the last 2 years?"

## PLANNING & PRODUCTIVITY

21. "Help me plan my day. Here's what I have: [LIST TASKS/MEETINGS]. Prioritize them and suggest a schedule."

22. "Do a weekly review with me. Ask me what I accomplished, what I didn't finish, and what's coming up next week."

23. "I want to achieve [GOAL] in the next [TIMEFRAME]. Break it down into weekly milestones with specific action items."

24. "I have these 10 tasks. Help me sort them by urgency and importance using the Eisenhower matrix: [LIST TASKS]"

25. "Create a project plan for [PROJECT]. Break it into phases with deliverables and rough timelines."

26. "Help me time-block my calendar for tomorrow. I have [X hours] of available time and these priorities: [LIST]"

27. "I'm overwhelmed. Here's everything on my plate: [BRAIN DUMP]. Help me make sense of it and figure out what to tackle first."

28. "Set up a habit tracker for me. I want to track: [LIST HABITS]. Create a simple daily checklist format."

29. "I have a deadline on [DATE] for [PROJECT]. Work backward and tell me what I need to do each day to hit it."

30. "Help me do a quarterly review. What should I reflect on? Ask me the right questions."

## CONTENT CREATION

31. "Write a video script about [TOPIC]. Structure: hook (5 sec), setup (15 sec), 3-4 main points (2 min), CTA (10 sec). Conversational tone, not scripted-sounding."

32. "Generate 10 hook ideas for a [PLATFORM] post about [TOPIC]. Each hook should stop someone mid-scroll."

33. "Write 5 Instagram captions for a [TYPE OF BUSINESS]. Mix educational, personal, and promotional. Include hashtag blocks."

34. "Turn this blog post into a Twitter/X thread (5-7 tweets): [PASTE CONTENT]"

35. "Create a content calendar for next week. I post on [PLATFORMS] about [TOPICS]. Mix content types: educational, personal story, promotional, engagement."

36. "Write a LinkedIn post about [TOPIC/EXPERIENCE]. Professional but not corporate. End with a question to drive comments."

37. "I have a [LENGTH] video about [TOPIC]. Give me 3 short-form clip ideas I can cut from it, with suggested captions."

38. "Write 5 headline options for my blog post about [TOPIC]. Optimize for clicks without being clickbait."

39. "Draft a YouTube description for my video about [TOPIC]. Include a summary, timestamps placeholder, and CTA for subscribing."

40. "Write a newsletter edition about [TOPIC]. Structure: hook, one main insight, 2-3 supporting points, CTA. Under 500 words."

## CAREER & PROFESSIONAL

41. "Tailor my resume for this job posting: [PASTE JOB DESCRIPTION]. Here's my current resume: [PASTE OR DESCRIBE]. Optimize for ATS keywords."

42. "Write a cover letter for [COMPANY/ROLE]. My relevant experience: [BRIEF SUMMARY]. Keep it under 300 words and mention something specific about the company."

43. "Prep me for an interview at [COMPANY] for [ROLE]. Give me: company research, 5 likely questions with STAR-method answers, and 3 smart questions to ask them."

44. "I got a job offer for [AMOUNT]. The market rate is [RANGE]. Draft a negotiation email that's confident but not aggressive."

45. "Review my LinkedIn profile and suggest improvements. Here's my current headline and summary: [PASTE]"

46. "Write a networking message to [PERSON] at [COMPANY]. I want to [YOUR GOAL]. Keep it under 50 words."

47. "Help me write my self-review for performance review season. My key accomplishments: [LIST]. Tone: confident but not arrogant."

48. "I want to transition from [CURRENT ROLE] to [TARGET ROLE]. What skills do I need? What's the gap? Create a 90-day learning plan."

49. "Review my portfolio/work samples and suggest what to add, remove, or improve: [DESCRIBE OR LIST]"

50. "Draft a professional bio for [CONTEXT — website, speaking, social media]. 100 words max. Here's my background: [SUMMARY]"

## BUSINESS & STRATEGY

51. "Create a one-page pitch for [BUSINESS/IDEA]. Include: problem, solution, target market, business model, competitive advantage, and ask."

52. "Help me price [PRODUCT/SERVICE]. My costs are [X], competitors charge [Y], and my target customer is [DESCRIPTION]. What should I charge and why?"

53. "Build a customer persona for [BUSINESS]. Include demographics, goals, pain points, where they hang out online, and what messaging resonates."

54. "Do a SWOT analysis for [BUSINESS/PROJECT]. Be honest — I want real weaknesses and threats, not just positive spin."

55. "Draft a partnership proposal to [COMPANY]. We can offer [YOUR VALUE]. We want [WHAT YOU NEED]. Make it a win-win."

56. "Help me model revenue for [BUSINESS]. Assumptions: [PRICE, CONVERSION RATE, TRAFFIC, etc.]. Show me monthly projections for 12 months."

57. "Create a launch checklist for [PRODUCT/SERVICE]. Cover: pre-launch, launch day, and post-launch. Don't let me forget anything."

58. "I'm deciding between [OPTION A] and [OPTION B] for my business. Help me think through it using a decision matrix with weighted criteria."
