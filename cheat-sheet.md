# Prompt Person™ — What to Say Cheat Sheet

Quick reference. Copy, paste, customize.

---

## Starting Your Day
- "Help me plan my day. Here's what I have going on: [list tasks/meetings]"
- "What's still on my plate from yesterday?"
- "Give me my top 3 priorities for today"
- "Run the daily briefing workflow"

## Writing Emails
- "Draft an email to [NAME] about [TOPIC]. Keep it under 100 words."
- "Write a follow-up to [NAME] — I haven't heard back in [X] days"
- "Make this email more professional: [paste draft]"
- "Draft a cold outreach to [COMPANY]. I want to [YOUR GOAL]."

## Doing Research
- "Research [TOPIC] and give me a 5-bullet summary"
- "Compare [OPTION A] vs [OPTION B] for someone who [YOUR SITUATION]"
- "What are the top trends in [INDUSTRY] right now?"
- "Fact-check this: [CLAIM]"

## Planning & Organizing
- "Break this project into steps: [DESCRIBE PROJECT]"
- "I'm overwhelmed. Here's everything on my plate: [BRAIN DUMP]"
- "Create a weekly schedule based on these priorities: [LIST]"
- "Help me set goals for the next 90 days"

## Building Something
- "Build me a [website/app/tool/script] that [WHAT IT DOES]"
- "Create a landing page for [MY BUSINESS/PROJECT]"
- "Make a tool that automates [REPETITIVE TASK]"
- "Help me build a [budget tracker/portfolio/dashboard]"

## When You're Stuck
- "I don't know where to start with [TASK]. Walk me through it."
- "Give me 5 different approaches to [PROBLEM]"
- "I've been working on this for hours. Review what I have and suggest next steps."
- "Help me make a decision: [OPTION A] vs [OPTION B]. My priorities are [LIST]."

## Remembering Things
- "Remember that [IMPORTANT FACT]"
- "What do you know about my client [NAME]?"
- "What did we work on last time?"
- "Save this for later: [INFORMATION]"

---

*Tip: The more specific you are, the better the output. "Draft an email" is ok. "Draft a 3-sentence follow-up to Sarah about the Q2 proposal, friendly but direct" is better.*
